Classic Italian Recipe

Lasagna

Layers of pasta, tomato sauce, cheese, and ground beef — baked until golden.

45
min prep
40
min bake
6
servings
Ingredients
Lasagna sheets 12 sheets
Tomato sauce 3 cups
Ground beef 500 g
Ricotta cheese 2 cups
Shredded mozzarella 2 cups
Parmesan cheese ½ cup
Basil or oregano 1 tsp
Garlic and onion to taste
Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions

  1. Prepare the filling

    Sauté the onion and garlic, then add the ground beef until browned. Add the tomato sauce and seasoning, and simmer for 15 minutes.

  2. Boil the lasagna sheets

    Cook the sheets according to package instructions until soft, then drain and lay them aside to prevent sticking.

  3. Make the cheese layer

    Mix the ricotta with half of the parmesan and a pinch of salt and pepper.

  4. Assemble the layers

    In a baking dish: a layer of sauce, then lasagna sheets, then the ricotta mixture, then mozzarella — repeat until ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and parmesan on top.

  5. Bake

    Cover the dish with foil and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for another 15 minutes until the cheese melts and browns.

  6. Rest and serve

    Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing so the layers set, then garnish with fresh basil and serve hot.
